What we deliver

Reporting modules that owners actually use

Every business is different — but the decision problems are usually the same. We build a pack that fits your model and stays consistent month to month.

Executive summary

Plain-English highlights: what moved, what’s normal, what needs attention, and what to do next.

KPI dashboard

Revenue, margin, net income, cash, runway, DSO, and a short list of weekly “watch metrics.”

Clean financial statements

P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow — structured to be readable and consistent as you grow.

Trend views and “what changed” breakdowns (mix, pricing, COGS drift, OpEx creep).

Spend analysis

Revenue quality

Implementation

How we set it up (then keep it consistent)

Most reporting fails because the foundation is messy. We clean the structure once, then run the same cadence every month.

Week 1 — Onboarding

Connect QuickBooks Online or Xero, confirm access, gather statements, align on KPIs and reporting preferences.

Week 2 — Cleanup & structure

Reconcile, correct categories, build a readable chart of accounts, and lock in a repeatable monthly close checklist.

Month 1 — Baseline pack

Deliver the first reporting pack (statements + dashboards + insights). Confirm what matters and iterate quickly.

Ongoing — Monthly close

Reconcile, review, and close with QA checks. We document anomalies so you’re never guessing.

Ongoing — Reporting cadence

You receive the reporting pack on a consistent schedule, plus a short summary explaining what changed.
